# 🏥 Andalusia Affiliate — Doctor App
A professional doctor referral platform designed for **affiliate doctors** to easily submit cases, receive pricing, and manage patient procedures within the Andalusia Hospitals network.
---
## 🚀 Overview
**Andalusia Affiliate** is a platform built specifically for doctors (not patients), enabling them to:
- Submit patient cases بسهولة
- Get fast and accurate pricing
- Schedule medical procedures
- Track case progress and payments
---
## ✨ Features
- 📸 Upload patient cases (images & documents)
- 🤖 AI-based medical data scanning
- 💰 Pricing within 15 minutes
- 📅 Easy procedure scheduling
- 🔔 Quick confirmation from call center
- 💳 Payment tracking system
---
## 🎯 Project Goal
To simplify collaboration between doctors and **Andalusia Hospitals** through a fast, reliable, and user-friendly system.
---
## 🧠 Concept
> **Fast. Simple. Reliable.**
The platform is designed to:
- Save doctors' time  
- Reduce complexity  
- Deliver a professional experience
